Loudly Crying
Info

A yellow face with an open mouth wailing and streams of heavy tears flowing from closed eyes. May convey inconsolable grief but also other intense feelings, such as uncontrollable laughter, pride or overwhelming joy. As of March 2021, the most popular emoji on Twitter. Loudly Crying Face was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
Apple

Emojis displayed on iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch and Apple TV use the Apple Color Emoji font installed on iOS, macOS, watchOS and tvOS. Some Apple devices support Animoji and Memoji. Two Private Use Area characters are not cross-platform compatible but do work on Apple devices.
