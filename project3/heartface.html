Heart Face
Info

A yellow face with smiling eyes, a closed smile, rosy cheeks, and several hearts floating around its head. Expresses a range of happy, affectionate feelings, especially being in love. The number of hearts (three or more) varies by platform. Apple's design features the same expression as its ☺️ Smiling Face. Smiling Face with Hearts was approved as part of Unicode 11.0 in 2018 under the name “Smiling Face with Smiling Eyes and Three Hearts” and added to Emoji 11.0 in 2018.
Apple

Emojis displayed on iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch and Apple TV use the Apple Color Emoji font installed on iOS, macOS, watchOS and tvOS. Some Apple devices support Animoji and Memoji. Two Private Use Area characters are not cross-platform compatible but do work on Apple devices.
